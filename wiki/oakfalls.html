Oak Falls

Oak Falls is a puzzle-based open world walking simulator which consists of several unlockable locations in series.

Gameplay

The player is introduced as a new electrical engineer in the massive Oak Falls power station, which powers the entire world off of nuclear fission. The player is given a handy multitool which holds several accessories that come in use later in the game. The game is divided into several areas around the world such as the Metro System and the Maintinence Sector that acts as individual maps that the player can freely walk around given that he/she has obtained entry into that area.

Plot

As the player gains access to more areas around the game, he/she slowly begins to discover hidden clues towards a conspiracy plot from a society of cult-like status called the 'Evolutionary Division' which promotes violently dealing with overpopulation in ways that align with 'survival of the fittest.' The group is attempting to release a secret pathogen developed under the reactor into the atmostphere to let the carriers survive. The player attempts to stop this action in the end of the game by blowing up the reactor and killing the biological weapon.
